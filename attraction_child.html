Local Attractions

The Village is situated at the foothills of the Darling Ranges and is only 50 minutes from the centre of Perth.

  • 10 minute drive to Byford Shopping Centre and Medical Centres
  • Less than 30 minutes to the Pinjarra, Mandurah, Rockingham and Armadale centres
  • Bus and train service nearby
