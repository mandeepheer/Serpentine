About Us

The park was built and opened in 1989 by a small local family. The same family still own and operate the park and also graze cattle and sheep on neighbouring land.

We had faith that the picturesque location of the park on the foothills of the Darling Ranges, the close distance to the stunning Serpentine Falls and National Park and the rural atmosphere would attract visitors.

Since the Park’s opening, we have seen the park grow and remain humbled by the compliments provided to us about the park as we pride ourselves on maintaining the park to a high standard.

We offer a range of accommodation options in order to cater for a broad array of visitors, including powered and unpowered sites, cabins and holiday homes.
